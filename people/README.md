# People

One folder per person who matters to your work: customers, prospects, advisors, collaborators.

```
people/
└── jane-doe/
    ├── profile.md     who they are, open loops, history
    ├── meetings/      notes from conversations
    └── briefings/     "brief me on Jane" outputs before a call
```

Say **"I met with Jane Doe"** or **"add person Jane Doe"** and Claude creates or updates the profile from `_templates/person.md`. Say **"brief me on Jane"** before a call and Claude writes a one-pager from everything in her folder.
