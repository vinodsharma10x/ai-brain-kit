# Wiki index

Table of contents for every page in this wiki. Maintained by Claude; updated on every ingest.

## How this wiki works

Two layers: you drop sources in `raw/`, Claude distills them into pages here. Pages carry YAML frontmatter and link to each other with [[wikilinks]]. You read this layer; you rarely need to write it.

Operations:
- **"ingest (file)"**: turn a raw/ source into new or updated pages
- **"what does the wiki say about X"**: synthesized answer with page citations
- **"lint the wiki"**: find contradictions, orphan pages, and missing concepts
