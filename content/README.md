# Content

Everything you publish, in two stages:

- `drafts/`: where Claude writes (say "draft a post about X"). Drafts are written in YOUR voice, loaded from `_context/voice.md`.
- `published/`: move a draft here when it ships, with a `published:` date and link in its frontmatter. This becomes the corpus future drafts learn from.

If a `founder-website/` folder sits next to this system in your workspace, finished blog posts can go straight into its `content/blog/` as MDX.
